Mission
Voxlens

VoxLens strives to provide premium smart glasses for visually impaired people to help them with their daily lives. With over 50 million people in the US with visual impairments, we aim to enhance their quality of life. Our glasses convert all essential visual information into audible information, enabling our users to lead successful lives, even with the challenges they face as visually impaired people.

AI Tech

VoxLens will be able to connect to modern devices using Bluetooth. Using well-developed software, we can translate all visual elements into audio.Our products utilizes advanced AI software's for object tracking and decision making for our users.

AI Assistant

VoxLens will use cameras and AI software to provide all sorts of visual information to our users through audio. Our product features a built in AI voice assistant for ease of use (Vox).

Vox App

The VoxLens App is a free user friendly app that allows users to harness OCR, AI technology, and key features through a smartphone interface. With an AI voicebot, our users will need little to no visual interactions. The Vox app will even allow for offline access and setting personalization for glasses, enhancing customer experience and loyalty.

Partners

Our product can connect to any modern device via Bluetooth to help users browse the internet and upload and access all sorts of information. For instance, imagine the user wants to email their boss about modifying their work schedule. Our user can ask Vox (the integrated AI-powered voice assistant) to complete the task.
